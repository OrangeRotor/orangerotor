Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in an aerial shoot?
Each shoot includes planning, flight time, and delivery of edited images or video tailored to your goals.

How long does it take to receive the final content?
Typically within 48–72 hours depending on the project scope. Rush delivery available upon request.

Can you fly in restricted airspace?
We’re certified and insured. We handle any necessary flight approvals to ensure legal and safe operations.

What format will I receive the media in?
Photos are delivered in high-resolution JPEGs, and videos in MP4. Custom file types available if needed.

Can I book recurring shoots for ongoing projects?
Absolutely. We work with builders, surveyors, and property managers on weekly and monthly schedules.
